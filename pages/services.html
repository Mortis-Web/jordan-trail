Our Services

Transportation

Private, air-conditioned transfers from airport to trailheads and between hike sections. Luggage transfer included.

Accommodation

A mix of wild camping, Bedouin camps, and local guesthouses. Experience authentic Jordanian hospitality.

Expert Guides

Certified wilderness first aid guides who are locals of the region. They are storytellers, cooks, and experts.

Food & Drink

Freshly cooked meals on the trail. Traditional Bedouin dishes like Zarb and Mansaf. Unlimited tea and water.

Equipment

High-quality tents, mattresses, and cooking gear provided. You just bring your sleeping bag and personal items.

Safety & Permits

We handle all reserve entrance fees (Dana, Petra, Wadi Rum) and coordinate with tourism police for safety.

Jordan Trail Adventures

Walking the ancient paths of Jordan with local Bedouin guides. More than a journey — a story written across Jordan’s landscapes.
