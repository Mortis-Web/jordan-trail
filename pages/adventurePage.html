Adventure Details

Dana to Petra

The Inca Trail of the Middle East

5 Days
72km
Hard

Overview

Rated by National Geographic as one of the best hikes in the world. This trek takes you through the biosphere reserve of Dana, along the mountain ridges of Wadi Araba, and into the ancient Nabataean city of Petra via the 'Back Door' monastery route.

1

Dana to Wadi Feynan

14km

Descent from the Mediterranean climate of Dana Village (1200m) to the desert landscape of Wadi Feynan (300m).

2

Wadi Feynan to Wadi Barwas

18km

A challenging day crossing the desert floor and ascending into the rugged mountains.

3

Wadi Barwas to Ras Al-Feid

16km

Climbing higher into the mountains with spectacular views of the Wadi Araba desert below.

4

Ras Al-Feid to Little Petra

14km

Passing through the sandstone mountains to the Neolithic site of Beidha and Little Petra.

5

Little Petra to The Monastery

10km

Entering Petra via the secret back route, arriving at the Monastery before descending to the Treasury.
